Developer Operations Engineer is an often overlooked role in software development, but it is a very important job. DevOps is responsible for ensuring the programmers' work is streamlined and simple mistakes such as deploying code that does not work, oes not happen on the job as it can cost the company a lot of money, time and damage the company reputation. Devops also do tasks like setting up the workspaces for software engineers to use and since this can be a tedious task, devops engineers usually use scripts to automate the tasks as much as possible.
